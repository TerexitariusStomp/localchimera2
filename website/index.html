How it works

Install. Write. Earn.

Three layers. One app window.

1

Install the app

One-line installer for macOS, Linux, and Windows. Docker compose for servers. The supervisor spins up the container automatically.

2

Write with AI

Open the LLM Wiki. Start typing. AI assists in real time. Auto-save every 2 seconds. Your knowledge base grows as you work.

3

Earn when idle

Connect your wallets. The node mines inference tasks in the background. Revenue streams to you while the device is idle.
